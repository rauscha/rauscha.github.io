I am Andrew Crane Rausch,

Obstetrics and Gynecology resident at Stamford Hospital

Now in my first year of residency, I’m interested in how advanced data-analysis can help guide physicians’ practice, and in high risk obstetrics.
Previously, I’ve researched subjects ranging from corn genomics, to galactic formation, to multi dimensional MRI image processing.
At home, I love to cook — I dare you to find a better braised short rib than mine!
Whether an interesting local beer, or a well made coffee, I dig well crafted drinks. Ask me, and I can talk about pour-over coffee for hours!
